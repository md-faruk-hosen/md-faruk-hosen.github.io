Welcome to My Website!

Hello! I’m Md. Faruk Hosen, currently working as a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Computing and Information Systems (CIS) at Daffodil International University.

I completed both my M.Sc. (Engg.) and B.Sc. (Engg.) degrees by achieving the 1st position in ICT from Mawlana Bhashani Science and Technology University, Bangladesh. I am the recipient of gold medals for ranking first in the engineering faculty during my B.Sc. (Engg.) studies, reflecting my academic excellence.

I am passionate about research and actively involved in projects that explore how technology can bridge communication gaps. My research interests include machine learning, deep learning, bioinformatics, health informatics, drug discovery and medical image processing.

I’m always eager to collaborate on innovative ideas and explore new opportunities. Please feel free to browse my website to learn more about my educational background, research, and professional journey. Thank you for stopping by!
